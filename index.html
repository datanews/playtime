Tips for Designing at Ludicrous Speed

Before Take-Off:

  • Let all your ideas spill out
  • Lay out and look at all your ideas together
  • Choose one idea that expresses your goal and is most achievable given time constraints

In Flight:

  • Keep a clear vision
  • Take one step at a time
  • Only make decisions that are consistent with your vision
  • Go!

Landing:

  • Done?! This was just a prototype!

  • Test it with three people who don't know what you are doing, find out what doesn't make sense to them.
  • Adjust Design, Take Off again
