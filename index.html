Playtime!

How quickly can you make something? Playtime is all about trying to quickly and playfully get something done. That something could be anything — a more fully-formed idea, a better title and mission for an existing project, a paper prototype of a news app, a drawing of a visualization, an interview process and actual interview, a quickly coded prototype, code tests for a project, a new logo, and so much more.
You have one hour.

Making at Ludicrous Speed

Before Take-Off

  • Let all your ideas spill out
  • Lay out and look at all your ideas together
  • Choose one idea that expresses your goal and is most achievable given time constraints

In Flight

  • Keep a clear vision
  • Take one step at a time
  • Only make decisions that are consistent with your vision
  • Test it with people who don't know what you are doing, find out what doesn't make sense to them.

Landing

  • DONE?! Maybe for now, but always adjust the design and take-off again and again.
